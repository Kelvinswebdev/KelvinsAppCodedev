    Why Choose Us

    Since our inception 20 years ago, Appcodehas built a model of education unlike any other that prepares you for a rewarding life and career. Eighty percent (80) of our students receive offers or start careers within four months of graduation, and our alumni are thriving across Africa and the world. Appcode has a history of academic excellence and has been consistent with training for over 20 years making us one of the leaders in the field. We’re renowned for our world-class research, global influence and impact across a broad range of program. Our reputation attracts the best and bravest of thinkers, who constantly cross boundaries, creating a home for thought leaders of today and tomorrow. Our diverse staff and student body and our location is in the heart of Accra. We recruit students all across Africa and work with global partners to teach them about the world. This means that Appcode gives you the best of both worlds: local roots with a pan-African network of classmates and a global experience that prepares you to thrive anywhere in the world.
